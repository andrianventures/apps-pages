About

Currency Conversion is our line of mobile apps for fast, practical currency conversion. Individual apps may focus on specific country pairs or features, but they share the same commitment to clarity and privacy described on this site.

Help

Depending on the app, you may get live or periodically updated exchange rates, a calculator-style interface, conversion history, and—in supported titles—optional camera or photo-based tools (for example, reading a price from a tag) so you spend less time typing.

Rates and tools are for everyday reference, not professional trading, tax, or legal advice. See our Terms of Use.

Permissions (when applicable)

Not every app requests every permission. Your device will only ask when a feature needs it.

  • Camera — only if you start an in-app scan or capture flow.
  • Photo Library — only if you choose an existing image for a scan or similar feature.

Subscriptions

Some apps may offer optional premium features through in-app purchase. On Apple devices, manage or cancel subscriptions in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
